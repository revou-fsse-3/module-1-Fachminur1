Who is Quby?

"Characterised by its rosy cheeks, abnormally huge head, and stick-like limbs, Quby (also known as Pentol) was created by Zhong Chaoneng in 2015 for the popular instant messaging platform WeChat. The first sticker pack was so successful that Zhong left his day job to become a full-time sticker designer."
